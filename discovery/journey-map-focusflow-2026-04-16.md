## Stage Details

### Stage 1: Discovery
**Trigger:** Sees a tweet: *"I put FocusFlow on my second monitor and I haven't opened Twitter in 2 hours"* or a r/productivity post: *"Replace your second screen wallpaper with this"*

**Actions:**
- Clicks link from social media / Google search
- Reads a brief description
- Expects: either a music app, a screensaver, or a Pomodoro timer

**Thoughts:**
- *"I already use Lofi Girl on YouTube but I have to deal with ads"*
- *"I've tried like 4 Pomodoro apps... none stick"*
- *"What makes this different?"*

**Emotional State:** 😐 Skeptically curious — fatigued by productivity tool hype

**Touchpoints:** Twitter/X, Reddit r/productivity, Google Search, referral from a colleague

**Pain Points:**
- ⚠️ Assumed: High skepticism — Arjun has tried multiple tools that didn't stick
- Discovery relies on strong word-of-mouth before SEO kicks in

**Opportunities:**
- Make the social proof copy specific: *"Built for your second screen"* hooks dual-monitor users immediately
- End-of-day card sharing creates organic discovery loop — Arjun *becomes* the distribution

---

### Stage 2: First Visit (Homepage)
**Trigger:** Lands on focusflow.app

**Actions:**
- Sees an ambient scene playing immediately in the hero (autoplay, muted)
- Reads the tagline: *"Your second screen, finally doing something beautiful"*
- Clicks "Try it free" — no sign-up required to preview the app
- Picks a scene from the hero carousel

**Thoughts:**
- *"Okay this actually looks different"*
- *"Can I just try it without creating an account?"*
- *"How is this different from just putting YouTube in the background?"*

**Emotional State:** 😮 Pleasantly surprised → 😊 Interested

**Touchpoints:** Landing page, hero scene preview, CTA

**Pain Points:**
- If sign-up is required before they can *feel* the product → immediate drop-off
- ❌ Critical risk: if the first scene loads slowly or looks generic, they leave
- ⚠️ Assumed: Users don't read feature lists — they need to *feel* the product in under 10 seconds

**Opportunities:**
- ✨ **Moment of Truth #1:** The first 10 seconds. If the scene is cinematic and audio starts softly → they're hooked. If not → they're gone.
- Anonymous "try before sign-up" mode is critical for Arjun
- Time-of-day scene variant that matches his actual current time creates instant magic

---

### Stage 3: First Focus Session
**Trigger:** Opens FocusFlow on second monitor, primary monitor has work open

**Actions:**
- Selects scene (default: auto-matched to current time of day)
- Picks audio preset (e.g., "Café + Lo-fi")
- Adds 3 sticky notes with today's tasks
- Starts Pomodoro (25 min)
- Works; hears soft chime at end of session

**Thoughts:**
- *"Actually this is quite calming"*
- *"I like that I can see my tasks without switching apps"*
- *"The chime didn't startle me — good"*
- *"I want to see what the other scenes look like"*

**Emotional State:** 😊 Focused and calm → 😌 Settled into flow

**Touchpoints:** Scene selector, audio controls, sticky note canvas, Pomodoro timer

**Pain Points:**
- Friction in scene selection if too many options are shown upfront (choice paralysis)
- ⚠️ Assumed: First sticky note setup might feel unfamiliar (drag anywhere)
- If audio volume defaults are wrong (too loud/quiet) → breaks immersion immediately

**Opportunities:**
- ✨ **Moment of Truth #2:** First Pomodoro completion chime. Soft, non-disruptive. This is the first "this works" moment.
- Onboarding tooltip: *"Drag sticky notes anywhere on the scene"* — shown once, dismissible
- Auto-selected scene based on time of day = zero-friction start

---

### Stage 4: Break Mode
**Trigger:** Pomodoro timer ends, break begins

**Actions:**
- Prompted: *"Time for a 5-min break. Lock your break?"*
- Clicks "Start Break"
- Full-screen ambient scene takes over with softer audio
- Countdown visible in corner
- Returns to focus mode at break end

**Thoughts:**
- *"I actually stepped away from my other screen"*
- *"This is the first time I've actually taken a proper break in weeks"*
- *"I feel like I'm... somewhere? Like I'm actually away from work?"*

**Emotional State:** 😌 Genuinely restful → 🤩 Surprised by how good the break feels

**Touchpoints:** Break Lock prompt, full-screen ambient scene, countdown timer

**Pain Points:**
- ⚠️ If Break Lock feels coercive or locks them OUT of their work monitor → frustration
- Must be easy to override if urgent

**Opportunities:**
- ✨ **Moment of Truth #3:** Break Lock. First time using it, Arjun experiences an actual break. This is the feature that turns FocusFlow from "nice ambient tool" into "essential work companion."
- *"Welcome back"* transition animation returning from break to focus scene

---

### Stage 5: End-of-Day Ritual (Clock Out)
**Trigger:** End of workday. Arjun clicks "End My Day" in the header.

**Actions:**
- Full-screen summary card animates in
- Sees: 6 Pomodoros · 8 tasks completed · 3 breaks taken · 3-day streak
- Reads a personalized message: *"3 days strong. You're building something real, Summit Seeker."*
- Option: Share card or close

**Thoughts:**
- *"I didn't realise I'd done 6 Pomodoros today"*
- *"I actually feel... done. Like I can close my laptop."*
- *"I want to screenshot this"*

**Emotional State:** 🤩 Proud and satisfied → 😄 Wants to share

**Touchpoints:** Clock Out CTA in header, summary card, share button

**Pain Points:**
- ⚠️ If the card looks clinical/data-heavy instead of beautiful → emotional moment is lost
- Streak counter must be prominent — it's the return hook

**Opportunities:**
- ✨ **Moment of Truth #4:** The End-of-Day card. This is the retention engine. It must feel like a gift — a moment of recognition, not a report.
- Share button generates a beautiful image card for Twitter/LinkedIn
- *"See you tomorrow"* with tomorrow's streak target creates anticipatory motivation

---

### Stage 6: Return & Habit Formation
**Trigger:** Next morning, opens laptop

**Actions:**
- FocusFlow is bookmarked / set as second-screen startup app
- Opens FocusFlow, sees streak counter: *"4-day streak 🔥"*
- Morning scene (sunrise variants) is auto-selected
- Starts the day with intent

**Thoughts:**
- *"I don't want to break the streak"*
- *"This is my morning ritual now"*

**Emotional State:** 😌 Motivated and grounded

**Pain Points:**
- No native desktop app → relies on browser/bookmark behaviour
- ⚠️ Streak anxiety if they miss a day

**Opportunities:**
- Streak shield mechanic to protect against anxiety
- "Morning mode" that auto-plays morning scene variants when opened between 6–10am
- Browser extension or PWA to set FocusFlow as second-screen default

### Stage 7: Advocacy
**Trigger:** Colleague asks why the second monitor looks so beautiful. OR Arjun tweets his End-of-Day card.

**Actions:**
- Shares end-of-day card: *"6 deep focus sessions, 8 tasks done, 3-day streak. FocusFlow is the tool I didn't know I needed."*

**Opportunities:**

# Moments of Truth — Cross-Persona Summary

*The 5 make-or-break moments that determine whether FocusFlow retains or loses a user.*

| # | Moment | Stage | Who It Affects | Current State | What Must Happen |
|---|--------|-------|----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **First scene load** | First Visit | All personas | ❌ Unbuilt | Scene must be cinematic and load in <2s. This is the product's first impression. |
| 2 | **First Pomodoro completion** | First Session | All personas | ❌ Unbuilt | Chime must be soft and satisfying — not jarring. First "it works" moment. |
| 3 | **First Break Lock** | Break | Arjun + Sam | ❌ Unbuilt | User must feel *actually transported*. If they dismiss it immediately, the feature fails. |
